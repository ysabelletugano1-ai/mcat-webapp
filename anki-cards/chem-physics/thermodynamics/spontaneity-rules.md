When is a reaction always/never spontaneous based on ΔH and ΔS signs?

---

| ΔH | ΔS | Spontaneous? |
|----|-----|--------------|
| − | + | **Always** (ΔG always negative) |
| + | − | **Never** (ΔG always positive) |
| − | − | Only at **low temperature** |
| + | + | Only at **high temperature** |
